Executive Readout

Revenue is highly concentrated.

The top 10 advertisers produce 17,020 of 18,074 revenue units and 93.96% of margin. The remaining 32 advertisers contribute 1,054 revenue units.

50 Partners is the clear leader.

It contributes 4,100 revenue units and 3,830 margin units, the highest absolute contribution in the portfolio.

Income 88 is the conversion engine.

Income 88 generated 134 sales, 65.4% of portfolio sales, but carries a lower 58.88% margin rate because payout is materially higher.

Quality risk is isolated but visible.

13Aff, Arzuno Partners, and 50 Partners show elevated proxy or hosting-click rates. These should be monitored before scaling spend.

Leadership Decisions

Defend the core

Prioritize relationship management and offer access for 50 Partners, Betti Affiliates, Income 88, and Vegas Legends. They represent 68.3% of total revenue.

Scale controlled tests

Arzuno Partners has high EPC and a 100% reported margin rate at smaller scale. Expand carefully with traffic-quality guardrails.

Review unit economics

Income 88 is the sales leader, but its payout level compresses margin. Review commission terms and product mix before aggressive expansion.

Clean the tail

The 32 advertisers outside the top 10 generate only 5.8% of revenue. Segment them into scale, nurture, and deprioritize groups.
